Format

Competition Format

The MATZON World Cup runs across three qualifying weeks via the Nations League, followed by Continental Championships and a Grand Final.

Nations League

Six regional zones compete simultaneously. Each zone runs group stages followed by knockout rounds across three weeks.

Continental Championships

Qualified nations advance to their respective continental championship, competing for spots in the MATZON World Cup Finals.
