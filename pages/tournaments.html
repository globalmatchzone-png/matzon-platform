Consistency ranking explanation

Throughout all three qualifying-weeks, national teams earn consistency points based on their performance across both the group stages and the knockout stages.

Direct qualification in week 3

A limited number of teams qualify directly through their performance in week 3. These spots are awarded to the top-performing teams of the final week.

Competition Format

The MATZON World Cup runs across three qualifying weeks via the Nations League, followed by Continental Championships and a Grand Final. Teams accumulate consistency points throughout the season.

Nations League

Six regional zones compete simultaneously. Each zone runs group stages followed by knockout rounds across three weeks.

Continental Championships

Qualified nations advance to their respective continental championship, competing for spots in the MATZON World Cup Finals.
